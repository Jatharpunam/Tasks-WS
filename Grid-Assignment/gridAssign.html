Rajgad Fort

Rajgad Fort is a historic hill fort near Pune, once the capital of the Maratha Empire.

Pratapgad Fort

Pratapgad Fort is a historic fort near Mahabaleshwar, famous for Shivaji Maharaj's victory over Afzal Khan.

Purandar Fort

Purandar Fort is a historic fort near Pune, known as the birthplace of Sambhaji Maharaj.

Shivneri

Shivneri Fort is the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

Sajjangad

Sajjangad is a historic fort near Satara, known as the final resting place of Sant Ramdas.
